# IEP Progress Monitoring Dashboard

## Problem
Special education teams require timely, data-informed insight into student progress toward Individualized Education Program (IEP) goals to ensure appropriate instructional adjustments and regulatory compliance.

This project focused on developing a structured dashboard to monitor academic and behavioral goal attainment, identify risk indicators, and support proactive intervention planning.

##Data
Synthetic, anonymized dataset simulating IEP goal tracking across academic, behavioral, and social-emotional domains.

Data included:
 - Goal-specific performance metrics
 - Behavioral frequency tracking
 - Skill acquisition benchmarks
 - Transition and classroom-specific behavior indicators

## Analytical Approach
- Cleaned and standardized multi-domain goal tracking data
- Aggregated performance trends across instructional settings
- Built a dynamic dashboard in Excel to visualize progress trajectories
- Identified students trending below expected growth benchmarks
- Conducted trend analysis across behavioral and academic indicators

## Key Findings
- Behavioral data indicated frequent elopement during mathematics instruction and transitions from math to specials, suggesting a subject-specific trigger pattern
- No statistically meaningful growth observed in emotional identification skill development over the measured period
- Positive upward trend observed in the use of replacement behaviors, indicating progress toward reducing inappropriate classroom behaviors

## Tools
Excel (data cleaning, aggregation, dashboard design, trend visualization)
